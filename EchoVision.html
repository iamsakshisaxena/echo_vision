ABOUT US

EchoVision

At EchoVision, we’re redefining how humans interact with technology. Our AI-powered voice assistant with an integrated camera isn't just smart — it sees, listens, and understands. Designed for the future, EchoVision helps you manage tasks, control your smart home, connect with others, and stay secure — all through a single voice command.

Our Mission

Millions of visually impaired individuals face daily challenges navigating unfamiliar environments.At EchoVision, our mission is to bridge that gap — by delivering an affordable, intelligent voice assistant with built-in visual awareness. We aim to empower users with seamless voice-guided support, bringing confidence, safety, and independence into their everyday lives.

Target Audience

Visually impaired individuals face challenges in navigating unfamiliar environments. Existing assistive tools are limited in real-time feedback and contextual awareness. There's a need for affordable, intelligent solutions that offer voice-guided help.EchoVision is designed to fit into your world and make it smoother, smarter, and more intuitive.
